Arnab Kishor Bordoloi Young Technovators Competition, 2021

Arnab Kishor Borboloi Young Technovator Competition: Roboworld launches its most prestigious annual Event "Arnab Kishor Bordoloi Young Technovator Competition" on 9th of September in the loving memory of our beloved alumini Arnab Kishor Bordoloi on the ocassion of his death anniversary.

Arnab Kishor Bordoloi was one of the most brightest and supportive alumni members Roboworld ever had. He was one of the founding members of the present Roboworld Club. On the darkest day of 9th September 2020, we lost Arnab Da during the tragedy of Baghjan fire.

To immortalise his contributions to JEC Roboworld and as an ode of respect and acknowledgement "Arnab Kishor Bordoloi Young Technovator Competition" will seek to appreciate the brightest young talents each year and encourage them to pursue brilliance, something which Arnab daa always aspired.
